About ICNSIET 2025

ICNSIET 2025 brings together academics, researchers, industry experts and students to present and discuss recent advances in networking, cybersecurity, embedded systems and Internet of Things applications. The conference welcomes original research articles, case studies and surveys that contribute to theory, applications and practice.

Who should submit?

Researchers, graduate students, industry practitioners and engineers working in related fields.

Publication

Accepted papers will be considered for proceedings and indexed in relevant publication channels (details available on acceptance).

Topics of Interest

Networking & Communication

5G/6G networks, SDN, network function virtualization, network automation and performance evaluation.

Security & Privacy

Network security, intrusion detection, secure protocols, privacy-preserving systems, applied cryptography.

Internet of Things (IoT)

IoT architectures, edge computing, sensor networks, low-power devices, industrial IoT applications.

Embedded & Cyber-Physical Systems

Real-time systems, reliable design, safety, hardware-software co-design, secure firmware.

AI & Edge Intelligence

ML for networking/IoT, federated learning, edge inference, anomaly detection and intelligent protocols.

Paper Submission (Microsoft CMT)

All submissions & peer-review management

ICNSIET 2025 uses Microsoft CMT for all paper submission, review and decision processes. Authors must create or use an existing CMT account to submit. Use the official conference page on CMT to submit abstracts and full papers.

  1. Prepare manuscript: Follow the conference template and page limits (check CMT page for template and instructions).
  2. Create CMT account: If you don't have one, register on Microsoft CMT.
  3. Submit on CMT: Upload your abstract/full paper on the official CMT conference page.
  4. Double-blind review: Papers undergo double-blind peer review as described on the CMT portal.

CMT documentation (website requirements & external website guidance) has been consulted. The conference web page clearly lists title, dates, venue, contact information and the official CMT link as required.
