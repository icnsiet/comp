About the Conference

The International Conference on Smart Innovations in Engineering and Technology (ICNSIET 2026) provides a global platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to exchange innovative ideas and explore advancements in engineering, computing, and intelligent systems.

The conference encourages interdisciplinary collaboration to address emerging challenges through sustainable and technology-driven solutions. All submissions and peer-review management are handled via Microsoft CMT.
