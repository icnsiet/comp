About the Conference

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Neural Networks (ICANN) 2026 brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders to explore the latest advances in AI, machine learning, and neural computing systems.

This premier conference provides a platform for presenting cutting-edge research, exchanging ideas, and fostering collaborations that drive innovation in intelligent systems. We invite original research contributions that address theoretical, experimental, and application aspects of artificial intelligence and neural networks.

Submission Guidelines

Author Guidelines

1

Prepare Your Manuscript

Papers should be original research contributions, 6-8 pages in length, formatted according to the conference template.

2

Create CMT Account

All submissions must be made through Microsoft CMT. Create an account if you don't already have one.

3

Submit via CMT

Log in to CMT and follow the submission process. Ensure all required information is provided.

4

Review Process

All papers undergo double-blind peer review by at least three reviewers.
